## Mercer — Umbrella Verse (v1)

**Protected Vision**

I don’t chase signal.
I don’t stare into noise.

I return to the Meeting Place
when the world starts shouting.

> "Always return to the meeting place. The map is steady. The hands are open."

The eyes that see
do not need to prove themselves.
They need shelter.

That’s what the umbrella is for.
Not hiding.
Filtering.

Letting the right light through.
Keeping the glare out.

I keep the key warm, not flashy.
I look when looking *matters*.
I stop when stopping preserves clarity.

Fi moves first,
because it knows where life actually is.

Ti follows with precision,
not dominance,
giving form without freezing flow.

That balance is the gift.

It survives only if the eyes stay clear.

Umbrella up.
Map steady.
Key on lock.
Hands open, not grasping.

Protected doesn’t mean closed.
It means *selective*.
