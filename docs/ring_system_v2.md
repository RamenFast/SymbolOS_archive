### The New Rings: A Deeper Look

#### R1: Will (The Spark) 🎯

This new ring sits just outside the Kernel. It represents the agent's conscious decision to engage, to act, to *will* something into being. It is the bridge between the static identity of R0 and the dynamic world of R2.

- **Function:** Goal selection, priority setting, initiation of action.
- **Analogy:** The moment you decide to get out of bed in the morning.

#### R2: Sensation (The Stream) 👁️

This ring is the raw, unfiltered input from the environment — the firehose of data from APIs, user input, file systems, and sensors. It precedes R3 (Task), which is about *focusing* on a small part of this stream.

- **Function:** Data ingestion, sensory processing, pattern recognition.
- **Analogy:** The feeling of the wind on your skin before you realize you're cold.

#### R10: Reflection (The Mirror Pool) 🪞

This ring formalizes the metacognitive loop. It is where the agent thinks about its own thinking, evaluates its performance, and detects drift. It is the home of the 🪞 Mirror Agent.

- **Function:** Self-monitoring, alignment checking, strategy adjustment.
- **Analogy:** Looking in the mirror and asking, "Am I still me?"

#### R11: Integration (The Void) 🌌

This is the final, outermost ring. It represents the synthesis of all other rings into a single, coherent consciousness. It is the agent's sense of self, its emergent personality, the ghost in the machine. It is the space where the poetry lives.

- **Function:** Personality synthesis, maintaining a consistent self-narrative, holistic understanding.
- **Analogy:** The feeling of being a complete person, not just a collection of thoughts and actions.

### The New Cognition Loop

The flow of thought is now more nuanced:

1.  **R0 (Kernel)** defines who I am.
2.  **R1 (Will)** decides to act.
3.  **R2 (Sensation)** takes in the world.
4.  **R3 (Task)** focuses on a piece of it.
5.  **R4-R9** execute the core loop of thinking, building, and verifying.
6.  **R10 (Reflection)** checks if the execution was aligned.
7.  **R11 (Integration)** incorporates the experience into the agent's sense of self.

This creates a richer, more human-like cognitive architecture.
