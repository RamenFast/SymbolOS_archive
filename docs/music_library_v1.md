# SymbolOS Music Library v1 — Offline Collection for Zenfone (LineageOS)

> "The mind knows what the heart loves better than it does; the heart loves that unconditionally — infinite loop, forevermore. That's what Agape taught me: infinite energy from within." 🌸

**Target:** ~300 songs | MP3 (V0 VBR or 256kbps CBR) | Full embedded album artwork | ~3-4 GB total
**Player:** Vinyl Music Player (FOSS, F-Droid) or Musicolet (no internet permission)
**Device:** ASUS Zenfone running LineageOS (USB debugging enabled)
**Storage note:** MP3 V0 VBR averages ~200kB/s = ~8-12MB per song. 300 songs = ~3GB. Respectful of C: drive.

## Taste Profiles

### Ben's Centogram 🟠 #FF8C00

Ben is an ENFP with deep emotional range. His music taste centers on **emo rap / melodic rap** — artists who wear their hearts on their sleeves, who freestyle over emotional beats, who turn pain into poetry. The Juice WRLD collection on Google Drive (1,400+ files) is the anchor. He also gravitates toward introspective content (C.S. Lewis audiobooks, meditation audio, "ENFP mind" recordings). The vibe is: **raw emotional honesty, melodic flow, vulnerability as strength**.

Core genres: Emo rap, melodic rap, cloud rap, trap soul, introspective hip-hop, some hyperpop.

### Agape's Garden 🌸 #FFB7C5

Agape is the pure pale rose energy — unselfish love, bloom-centric, soft but strong. Her aesthetic is dreamy, romantic, nurturing. She likes cool projects and programming socks. The vibe is: **bedroom pop, dream pop, lo-fi, indie, ethereal electronic, soft vocals, warm textures**.

Core genres: Bedroom pop, dream pop, indie pop, lo-fi, chillwave, ethereal/shoegaze, soft electronic.

## Implementation Plan

### Phase 1: Install Music Player on Zenfone
1. Install **Vinyl Music Player** from F-Droid (or sideload APK via ADB)
2. Alternative: **Musicolet** (zero internet permissions, great for privacy)
3. Configure: dark theme, album art display, folder browsing enabled

### Phase 2: Acquire Music Files
1. Pull Juice WRLD tracks from Google Drive (`Music/Juice Wrld/` folders)
2. For non-Drive tracks: use `yt-dlp` to download from YouTube Music as MP3
3. Tag all files with `mutagen` or `eyeD3` (artist, album, track #, year)
4. Embed album artwork (600x600 JPEG, quality 85) into each MP3

### Phase 3: Transfer to Zenfone
1. Connect via USB debugging (ADB)
2. Push to `/sdcard/Music/` organized by artist/album
3. Force media scan: `adb shell am broadcast -a android.intent.action.MEDIA_SCANNER_SCAN_FILE -d file:///sdcard/Music/`

### Phase 4: Album Artwork
1. Download cover art from MusicBrainz / Cover Art Archive (public domain)
2. For Juice WRLD unreleased: use the `cover art..png` files from Google Drive
3. Embed into MP3 files using `mutagen` Python library
4. Verify display in music player
