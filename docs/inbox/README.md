## Scroll of Conventions 📜 🔴 #FF2400 (righteous boundary)

To ensure your messages are not lost to the ether, follow these ancient rites:

- **Date Prefix**: Begin each filename scroll with the date of its creation: `YYYY-MM-DD_...`
- **Preferred Medium**: Inscribe your words in the common tongue of Markdown.
- **Provenance Header**: At the top of each scroll, declare its origin:
  - **Source**: Where did this knowledge come from? (Drive/GitHub/etc)
  - **Owner**: Who is the keeper of this knowledge?
  - **Date**: When was this knowledge first recorded?
  - **Classification**: How secret is this knowledge? (public/internal/confidential/restricted)
