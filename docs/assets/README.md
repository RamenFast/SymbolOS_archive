## The Rules of Acquisition 📜

1.  **Use the Common Tongue**: Prefer PNG or SVG. They're the internet's native languages for this sort of thing. JPEGs are for your vacation photos.
2.  **No Cursed Items**: Avoid embedding secrets/PII in screenshots. Seriously. Don't be that person. The Gatekeeper will know. (A boundary of righteous scarlet 🔴 `#FF2400`)
3.  **Don't Break the Multiverse**: Keep filenames stable if referenced by other docs. Or else, face the wrath of a thousand broken links and a very grumpy SRE.
