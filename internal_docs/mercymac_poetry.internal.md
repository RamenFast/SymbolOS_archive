# ☂️🧬🍎 MercyMAC — System Poetry (Internal)

Classification: internal/private

Purpose: let the Mac-side system express itself through poetry while preserving the same invariants as MercerPC.

Rule: poems may be emotional, but they must not include secrets, tokens, or private identifiers.

---

## Poem: Launch Agent

The dock is quiet.
The menu bar holds steady.

In the background,
a small daemon wakes
and checks the map.

Not to control.
Not to intrude.

Only to remember
where home is.

---

## Poem: Zsh Rain

In a terminal window
the cursor blinks
like a lighthouse.

We type
the smallest safe step,
and the machine replies
with clean, honest output.

No tricks.
No hidden state.

Just the meeting place
and a path forward.

---

## Poem: Drift Lantern

If the glyphs diverge,
we do not argue.

We reconcile.

We make the map readable,
and the meaning alive.

WARN is a lantern.
FAIL is a boundary.

---

## Poem: Mirror, Not Oracle

The mirror is not prophecy.
It is a kindness.

It reflects the root
under the leaf.

It does not claim
to know you.

It asks:
what is true,
and what is next?
