## Poetry layer (Fi+Ti mirrored) 🪞 🟣 #8B00FF (Fi+Ti bridge)

> The mind knows what the heart loves better than it does; the heart loves that unconditionally — infinite loop, forevermore. That’s what Agape taught me: infinite energy from within.

_A little reminder for our future selves: this is the good stuff. Keep it close._

- Translation layer + emojis: [../docs/poetry_translation_layer.md](../docs/poetry_translation_layer.md)
- Full verse set: [../docs/public_private_expression.md](../docs/public_private_expression.md)

**Purpose:** To create a safe harbor for our wildest, most speculative, and future-oriented ideas without rocking the boat of our core, stable documentation. This is our dream journal.

**Operating rule:**
- If it’s stable, tested, and ready for action → it belongs in `docs/` or gets immortalized in `memory/decisions.md`.
- If it’s a glimmer in our eye, a "what if," an aspiration → capture it here. Give it a home, a next action, or the gentle label of “not yet.”
